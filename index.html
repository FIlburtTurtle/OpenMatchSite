About

Urban Developers is a developer and manager of retail and residential mixed-use
projects in highly populated urban areas with a focus on transit-oriented sites.

Mission

To conceive, design and execute transit-oriented, sustainable, mixed-use village nodes in large urban settings. so people can live, shop and play with satisfaction and security.

Purpose

To acquire and build institutional-quality projects that are sustainable, well-located, and economically sound.

Focus

To introduce new development or adaptive use of existing assets to urban locations in metro areas of San Francisco, Los Angeles, and San Diego, where it will be transformative to the neighborhood.

What We Do

Addressing and respecting the divergent needs of retailers and residents.

The key in large-scale mixed-use development is delivering a finished product that is exciting for all stakeholders, from the retail anchor stores to the stand-alone storefronts to the residents. We feel that it’s part art and part science, that balancing the demands to create a truly acceptable site is the result of decades of experience.Our relationships with large box retail tenants allows us to start the process of design based on the demands and requirements of an anchor tenant use. We add to that the experience with vertical multifamily development on small urban sites to solve the complexities of access, security and exceptional views for the residential component. As municipalities trend toward requiring mixed-use to densify their downtowns, we are approached by retailers who control sites and want an experienced developer to add the residential component in an economic and sensi- tive design on the site.

ALAN D. DIBARTOLOMEO

Alan Dibartolomeo, president of A&D Investment Group, Inc., brings 30 years of acquisition, development and management experience to the institutional grade real estate entities he directs.

In the 2000’s, Mr. Dibartolomeo served in-house as Development/Construction advisor for Legacy Partners Residential Irvine, responsible for a seasoned team of professionals overseeing project completion of four apartment communities totaling over 900 units. In the business cycles encompassing the 1980s and 1990s, Mr. Dibartolomeo acquired, built teams and managed over a million square feet in Southern California in posi- tions with two of the most highly regarded commercial developers in the country, Trammell Crow Company and Spieker Properties. He has a wide range of experience encompassing housing, oce, industrial and retail entitlement and development.

Mr. Dibartolomeo has been a guest lecturer at the USC Masters in Real Estate Development graduate pro- gram, the USC Ross Minority Program in Real Estate, and Urban Land Institute presentations. Mr. Dibar- tolomeo has an undergraduate degree from UCLA and an MBA from Harvard University. He is married with three children, and resides in Orange County, California.
