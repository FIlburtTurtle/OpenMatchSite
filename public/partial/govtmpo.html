OpenMatch was created with the intention of providing effective rideshare software for government MPOs. In our pursuit to aggregate more rideshare data, we found that the software systems most MPOs were using were outdated and inadequately maintained. We believe that our ridematch software can help communities pool carpools together to reach a critical mass.
