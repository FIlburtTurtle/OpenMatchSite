About

OpenMatch’s mission is to improve the state of the carpooling market by giving individuals and organizations access to free open sourced ridematching software that can be customized for any situation. By actively maintaining, updating, and create modules for the software we hope to create a suite of tools for use in transportation demand management, mainly through our own efforts but also with the help of the community.

History

OpenMatch is a project of toEverywhere, an aggregator of carpools and rideshares, and we hope that through OpenMatch we can help seed more ridesharing platforms to partner with us and hopefully contribute back to the aggregator's mobility network.
