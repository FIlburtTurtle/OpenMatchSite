Features

Below are some of OpenMatch's current features.

We're still in alpha—new features coming soon!

Userbased Ridematching

Let your users create an area around their route where they are willing to pick up passengers.

Build in safety features

OpenMatch can easily import another database using a simple CSV dump of your current system.

Easy to install and implement

While not necessary, OpenMatch instances can easily feed to the toEverywhere aggregator, and have the capability to also pull data from it. This way you contribute to a large network of rides that benefit the TDM community as a whole.

Completely customizable

Setup, maintain, and iterate on your ridematch software for a fraction of the price of an inhouse team or other service.

Embed Friendly Interfaces

OpenMatch can easily import another database using a simple CSV dump of your current system.

Mobile friendly design

Works on mobile, table, and desktop
